4 · Payments are non-custodial

You pay directly from your own wallet by signing on-chain transactions. We never hold your funds and never have access to your keys. Funding a deployment forwards your payment in the same transaction; nothing is escrowed or held on your behalf. The “balance” a deployment shows is prepaid runtime - an accounting number on a public smart contract - not money in an account.

Blockchain transactions are irreversible, and so are payments here: all payments are final. There are no refunds, chargebacks, or reversals, and prepaid runtime cannot be converted back into funds. Runtime you have paid for is consumed as your deployment runs; if a runner fails mid-lease, up to one lease interval of prepaid time can be lost. Double-check the app, the shares, and the amount before you sign - the chain will not give it back.
