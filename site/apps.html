Enclave app store: deploy WebAssembly apps on confidential GPUs

Browse ready-to-run apps and launch any of them on private, confidential hardware in a couple of clicks. Pay for exactly the time you use - no setup, no subscription. And everything here is open: an app's code and listing can't be swapped out from under you, and you can verify what you're running yourself, before it touches your data. Built something of your own? Deploying is deliberately simple: pick an app from the store, set two dials - a GPU share and a CPU share - and fund it from your wallet. Hit Deploy and a confidential enclave claims the work, boots your app, and hands back its URL in seconds - the provisioning output and your running apps live on your Dashboard. Your wallet is your account - no servers, no lock-in: it's one call to the public REST API, shown here exactly as it goes out, and Dry run lets you inspect everything before spending anything.
