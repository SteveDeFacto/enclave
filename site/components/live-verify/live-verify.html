Don't take this page's word for it: run the check now

Your browser loads Tinfoil's open-source verifier (@tinfoilsh/verifier), fetches the enclave's attestation over your own connection, validates the AMD SEV-SNP report up to AMD's root key, verifies the release's Sigstore code provenance, and compares the two measurements. Exactly what the API's verify field tells every client to do.

    The verifier module loads from esm.sh; Sigstore bundles and AMD KDS certificates come through Tinfoil's CORS proxies. Fully independent path: tinfoil attestation verify -e <enclave-host> -r <repo>, via tinfoil-cli.
