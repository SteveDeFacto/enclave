volId = keccak256(owner, name) - unforgeable, survives failover
revoke() stops future unlocks but cannot claw back an already-unsealed key - real revocation is volume rotation. Open-enrollment volumes (autoGrant) re-admit anyone who registers.
Trust model. Your vault key derives from a deterministic wallet signature and exists only in this page's memory. The volume key (VEK) travels only sealed: on-chain it is sealed to your wallet-derived key; to an enclave it is sealed to a per-boot key you fetch over TLS after verifying the enclave's attestation - so neither the platform operator nor Tinfoil can ever decrypt. Reloading this page forgets everything.
Serving note. For maximum assurance, load this app from its attested enclave origin (https://<enclave-host>/x/<deployment>/) rather than a gateway origin - the enclave terminates TLS inside the measured CVM, so the page bytes themselves are enclave-authentic.
